About Us

Xander’s is a modern gourmet café – the concept is all about simple, fresh ingredients & light meals. You can grab a quick open-faced sandwich, a bowl of pasta, an ‘old school cheeseburger’ or a delicious dessert along with the all day breakfast that we will be serving.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

The cafe’s ambience is minimalistic and vibrant. With Indoor and outdoor seating, WI-FI and the latest lounge music, Xander’s promises to be the perfect place for the city’s younger and older discerning customers to get together.
